# Product Vision — SpartaFlow Hub

## 1. Overview

SpartaFlow Hub is the internal **Employee Operating System** of SpartaFlow, a 100% remote software company. It is not a task manager and is not a replacement for ClickUp. ClickUp continues to own task and project execution. SpartaFlow Hub owns everything *around* execution: attendance, daily workflow, cross-department communication, dependency tracking, reporting, and operational visibility.

## 2. Purpose

To give every employee a single, predictable place to:

- Start their working day.
- Know exactly what is expected of them at each moment of the day.
- Communicate progress, blockers, and dependencies.
- End the day with a structured report.

And to give every manager, HR member, and the Owner a single, real-time lens into:

- Who is working right now.
- Who is late, on break, or off.
- Where work is blocked and which department is blocking it.
- How departments, teams, and individuals are performing over time.

## 3. Vision Statement

> *To build the best internal operating system for a modern remote software company — one that removes ambiguity from the workday, surfaces blockers before they become risks, and turns operational visibility into a competitive advantage, without ever feeling like surveillance.*

## 4. Product Principles

1. **Operational, not executional.** SpartaFlow Hub orchestrates the day; ClickUp executes the tasks.
2. **Structured by default.** Check-ins, statuses, and reports follow a consistent shape so data is comparable across people, teams, and time.
3. **Visibility without micromanagement.** Managers see signals, not keystrokes. Employees see what's expected, not what's being watched.
4. **Remote-first.** Asynchronous workflows are the primary mode; synchronous interactions are optional.
5. **Low friction.** Daily flows must be completable in under 2 minutes total per day per employee.
6. **Trust the employee.** Defaults respect autonomy; rules exist to protect the team, not to police it.
7. **Single source of operational truth.** Attendance, reports, and dependencies live here — never in chat threads.

## 5. Scope Boundaries

**In scope:** attendance, working-day workflow, morning check-in, midday status, end-of-day report, dependencies between departments, announcements, notifications, manager and HR dashboards, performance analytics, employee directory, audit logs, roles and permissions.

**Out of scope:** task management, sprint planning, time-tracking per task, code review, design handoff, customer-facing features, payroll, recruiting ATS.

## 6. Strategic Outcome

When SpartaFlow Hub is successful, a new employee on day one understands the rhythm of the company within an hour, a manager understands the state of their team within 30 seconds of opening the dashboard, and the Owner understands the health of the company within a single screen.
